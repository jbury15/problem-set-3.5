Now, the year is 2018, and Super Bowl 53 will shortly be upon us. According to oddsshark.com, the five teams who have the best odds to win the Super Bowl are the Los Angeles Rams, the Kansas City Chiefs, the New England Patriots, the New Orleans Saints, and the Jacksonville Jaguars. From that list, only one team, the Los Angeles Rams, were one of the original 10 franchises in the NFL, after the merge. Professional football in the USA has come a long way, since the introduction of the first eleven professional teams, to the AFL NFL merger, the inclusion of new franchises, and the game has not stopped growing. There have recently been talks of having a professional american football team in england, and although it seems unlikely, based on how the sport has grown over the last 200 years, anything is possible.
